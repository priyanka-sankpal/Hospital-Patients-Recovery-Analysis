## Hospital-Patients-Recovery-Analysis :hospital:
 ### Project Overview: :pushpin:

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze patient recovery performance across hospital departments, treatment types, doctors, and gender.

The dashboard converts raw healthcare data into actionable insights that help hospital management monitor recovery trends, optimize treatment strategies, and improve operational decision-making.

 #### Business Problem :warning:

Hospitals generate large amounts of patient data, but identifying the factors that directly impact patient recovery can be difficult without proper analytics.

Management needed a centralized dashboard to:

-Track recovery performance across departments.

-Identify departments with high patient load.

-Monitor treatment effectiveness.

-Compare doctor-wise recovery outcomes.

-Analyze treatment costs and hospital stay duration.

-Improve patient care through data-driven decisions.

#### Solution Approach: :dart:

Built a healthcare analytics dashboard in Power BI that provides a complete view of patient recovery performance using KPI tracking and interactive visual analysis.

The dashboard helps stakeholders quickly identify:

-Which departments have the highest patient visits.

-Which treatment types lead to better recovery scores.

-Which doctors consistently perform better.

-Areas where operational improvements are required.

-Cost and recovery relationships for better healthcare planning.

 ##### Key KPIs
 
KPI	Purpose:

-Total Patients	Tracks overall patient volume

-Avg Recovery Score	Measures treatment success rate

-Avg Hospital Stay	Identifies hospitalization efficiency

-Avg Treatment Cost	Monitors healthcare spending

-Avg Patient Age	Understands patient demographics

 ##### Key Insights Generated :mag:
Department Performance Analysis:

-Identified Gastroenterology as the most visited department, helping management understand high operational demand areas.

 Recovery Trend Analysis:

-Compared recovery scores across departments and treatment types to identify better-performing healthcare processes.

 Doctor Performance Monitoring:

-Analyzed doctor-wise recovery scores to highlight consistently strong performers and support quality improvement initiatives.

Treatment Effectiveness Analysis:

-Measured how different treatment methods impact recovery outcomes for smarter treatment planning.

 Cost & Recovery Monitoring:

-Tracked average treatment cost alongside recovery performance to support efficient healthcare resource allocation.
